The Emily Jones

I am a Los Angeles based, enthusiastic digital project/client service manager with an edge in dev/design. I’m a creative problem solver who works hard, and plays works harder but somehow still don’t take myself too seriously. In my work I value humor, collaboration, organization, pixel perfection and on-point GIF responses. I was the Hermione Granger you probably hated in school, and now as a young professional I am still as tenacious and tireless as ever. And finally have the bushy hair under control (phew).

I’m a full time cat lover, gamer and temporary tattoo enthusiast. When I’m not working I enjoy attending Comic Conventions (WonderCon, SDCC and RTX are favorites), tracking down the best latte in Los Angeles, and pretending Parks and Recreation isn’t over.
